Who We Are

A team built on expertise and genuine care.

We don't just hire credentials — we hire people. Every member of our clinical team must meet two standards: advanced clinical training and a genuinely warm, caring personality. You can teach technique. You can't teach someone to care.

Our therapists collectively hold specialties in manual therapy, balance and vestibular rehab, Parkinson's care, orthopedics, acupuncture, and more — all under one roof across four Clackamas County locations.
